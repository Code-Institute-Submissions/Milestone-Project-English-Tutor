About Me:

My name is Jeanette. I live in the Alsace, France with my husband, children and springer spaniel dog. I am passionate about family, food, the environment and lifelong learning. My hobbies include reading, gardening, yoga, badminton and skiing. I have had quite a varied career history: After graduating with a degree in Biochemistry, I worked in pharmaceuticals for a few years before taking some time out to volunteer in the rain forest in Brazil. During this time I became interested in sustainability and so on my return I took an internship in development and sustainability which offered on the job training and qualifications and I went on to work for a decade in the field of community development. For the past 7 years I have been living in France and for the last 5 I have been teaching English. I am a native English speaker from Manchester England and I teach all levels from A1 to C2 to business men and women, retirees looking to travel, students preparing exams and people who just want to learn for the pleasure of learning.
